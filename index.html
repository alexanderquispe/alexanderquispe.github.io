About me

I am a Junior Researcher at the World Bank in the ID4D team and Lecturer in the Department of Economics at Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú (PUCP). Currently, I collaborate with Professor Victor Chernozhukov on the creation of Econometric Packages and Tutorials about Causal Inference and Machine Learning (CI & ML).

I received my B.S. in Economics at PUCP and my M.S in Quantitative Economics at the University of Munich. During my second year, I visited the Laboratory for Innovation Science at Harvard (LISH). I worked as RA at Yale, the Max Planck Institute for Innovation and Competition (MPI) and MIT Sloan.
